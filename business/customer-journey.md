# Customer Journey Map for Polyglot Bridge

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event:** Developers encounter challenges integrating multiple programming languages in their projects.
- **Friction Points:** Lack of awareness about existing solutions; confusion over the complexity of language integration.
- **User Emotions:** Frustration and confusion; feeling overwhelmed by the integration process.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Educational content (blogs, webinars) on the benefits of seamless multi-language integration; targeted ads highlighting success stories.
- **Metric:** Number of impressions and engagement rates on educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event:** Developers start researching solutions for multi-language integration.
- **Friction Points:** Overwhelming number of options; difficulty in comparing features and benefits; skepticism about claims made by competitors.
- **User Emotions:** Cautious optimism; curiosity mixed with doubt.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Clear comparison charts; testimonials and case studies from early adopters; interactive demos showcasing ease of use.
- **Metric:** Number of website visits and demo requests.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event:** Users sign up for a free trial or demo of Polyglot Bridge.
- **Friction Points:** Initial setup complexity; lack of clear onboarding instructions; potential bugs or performance issues during trial.
- **User Emotions:** Excitement mixed with anxiety; hope for a solution but fear of technical difficulties.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Comprehensive onboarding tutorials; responsive customer support during the trial; quick wins that demonstrate value.
- **Metric:** Trial conversion rate and user engagement metrics (e.g., time spent on platform).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event:** Users decide to purchase a subscription after the trial.
- **Friction Points:** Concerns about pricing; uncertainty about long-term value; potential integration with existing workflows.
- **User Emotions:** Relief and satisfaction; confidence in their decision, but still cautious about commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Flexible pricing plans; personalized onboarding sessions; community support forums for ongoing assistance.
- **Metric:** Subscription conversion rate and customer satisfaction scores (CSAT).

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event:** Users explore additional features or integrations after successful adoption.
- **Friction Points:** Limited awareness of advanced features; potential integration challenges with other tools.
- **User Emotions:** Enthusiasm for growth; desire to maximize the value of the product; occasional frustration with limitations.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Regular updates on new features; personalized recommendations based on usage; loyalty rewards for referrals.
- **Metric:** Upsell rate and net promoter score (NPS).
